Los angeles

Los Angeles, often abbreviated as L.A., is the largest city in California and the second-largest city in the United States, after New York City. It is known for its diverse population, cultural significance, and its association with the entertainment industry. L.A. enjoys a Mediterranean climate with mild, wet winters and hot, dry summers.
